Something tasty and quick for breakfast

Mekicii with yogurt and sparkling water

Necessary products:

  • eggs - 3 pcs.
  • yogurt - 400 g
  • carbonated water - 100 ml
  • baking soda - 1 tsp.
  • salt - 1 tsp
  • oil - 3 tbsp.
  • flour - about 1 kg
  • oil - 300 ml for frying

How to prepare it:

  1. Add baking soda to the yogurt, wait for it to activate and add it to the beaten eggs.
  2. Add salt, oil, carbonated water and sifted flour.
  3. A soft dough is kneaded. Leave covered with foil for 30 minutes.
  4. The dough is cut into balls and with greased hands, they are formed into soft balls, which are fried in hot oil on both sides until done.
